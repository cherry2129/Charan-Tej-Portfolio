Who I am

Thoughtful
by design.

I’ve always been drawn to the small details people usually overlook. The way something moves. The way a screen feels. The way good design quietly guides someone without asking for attention.

Right now, I’m pursuing B.Tech in Computer Science while exploring the space between design, technology, and storytelling. Most of my work comes from curiosity, observation, and a genuine interest in how people interact with digital experiences.

Over time, through branding projects at my previous internship and personal explorations, I’ve realized that the work I enjoy most is work that feels thoughtful, intentional and deeply human.
